Fried Rice

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking wine
  • 2 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Â½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 cups leftover white or brown rice

Procedure

  1. pre heat your pan/wok
  2. put 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil into the pan
  3. wait until the oil is hot enough before putting in eggs
  4. scramble the eggs well before putting 1/2 cups of frozen green peas in
  5. after cooking for 30 seconds, put in 2 cups of leftover rice and make sure there are no clumps
  6. put 2 tablespoon of soy sauce and mix thoroughly
  7. put 1 tablespoon of cooking wine and mix(optional)
